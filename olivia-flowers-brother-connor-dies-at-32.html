Olivia Flowers Brother, Conner, Dead At 32

 Posted on February 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  464 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Tragic breaking news as Southern Charm star Olivia Flowers’ brother, Conner, has been confirmed dead at the tender age of 32. Have any details related to Conner’s cause of death been revealed yet? Keep reading for all the details.

Southern Charm: Olivia Flowers’ Brother, Conner, Dead At 32

Speaking to Page Six, the Charleston County Coroner, Bobbi Jo O’Neal, confirmed Olivia Flowers’ brother Conner passed away on Monday, January 30th, 2023. He was at his home in the Isle of Palms outside Charleston, SC at the time of his death. The coroner told the media outlet that both the manner and cause of death of the Southern Charm star’s brother are pending and the local police are still investigating what happened.

Also speaking to Page Six, a Mount Pleasant, SC funeral home confirmed there would be a visitation for Conner on Saturday and a funeral on Sunday. While the Legacy obituary page has not been updated just yet, there is an entry from Olivia Flowers’ co-star Taylor Ann Green’s mother.

Precious Olivia, Robin and Garry… please know how heartbroken we are for your unspeakable loss. We are holding you in our hearts and prayers for the peace, comfort and strength of God to carry you through. With much love, Leslie and Rick Green (& all of Taylor Ann’s family).”

Southern Charm fans rush to support Olivia

Now, Olivia Flowers has not broken her silence on her brother’s passing at the age of 32. But, that hasn’t stopped fans from rushing to her most recent post on social media with their condolences.  Here’s some of what her Instagram followers have had to say:

  • “Olivia so sorry for the loss of your brother. Words cannot describe how our hearts hurt for you, your parents and family. know you are in our prayers for strength.”
  • “I’m so sorry for your loss. Sincere condolences to you and your family.”
  • “I am so sorry for your loss. Your brother Connor was such a great guy. Way to young”
