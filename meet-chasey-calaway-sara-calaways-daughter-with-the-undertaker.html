Who Is Chasey Calaway’s Mother?

Chasey Calaway’s mother is Sara Calaway. She was born Sara Chirie Frank on 21st July 1977. She is a retired American model, professional wrestler, and wrestling valet. Sara grew a passion for wrestling while she was in high school and was nicknamed The String One by her classmates who were amazed by her strength. She started her professional wrestling career in 2001 with King of the Ring and managed to achieve a certain level of popularity after some time.

Sara Calaway became more famous after she married The Undertaker who was already an established wrestler even before the time of their marriage. Her fame increased the more after an alleged stalker released several footage of her wrestling online. In the 2001 and 2002 WWE, she appeared as a valet for her ex-husband, The Undertaker.

Chasey’s father, Mark William Calaway, is an American retired professional wrestler widely regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time. Born on 24th March 1965, Mark started wrestling for WCCW in 1987. He was well known for his macabre, zombie image which won him Best Gimmick 5 years in a row.

The Undertaker retired in November 2020, having wrestled for 33 years, and became one of the most famous figures of the WWF/boundary-pushing E’s generation.
