Reese Witherspoon's little boy is growing up so fast!

The Walk the Line star took to Instagram on Tuesday, July 5 to share a sweet photo of herself with her three-year-old son Tennessee — and we're loving the rare snapshot of the two!

"Good 'ol #RedWhiteAndBlue! ?? (Post-nap festivities ?) Hope you had a relaxing holiday, folks!! ❤️ #FourthOfJuly," the 40-year-old actress captioned the sweet pic.
