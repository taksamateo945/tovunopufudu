Kylie Jenner Pets - Celebrity Pets

 Posted on April 30, 2024  |  11 minutes  |  2185 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Penny (Dog)

Breed: Beagle | Adopted Aug 2016

Meet Penny, a new puppy adopted into the Kylie Jenner clan in 2016. Her then BFF, Jordyn Woods, found the perfect gift for her 19th birthday in the form of an adorable beagle puppy. Now, Jordyn Woods is out after Tristan Thompson cheated on Khloe Kardashian with her, but Penny’s still part of the Jenner family. Well documented on Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter, her entire canine lifespan can be found on the Jenner social media pages.

Rolly (Dog)

Breed: English Bulldog | Adopted Sep 2015

In September 2015, Kylie Jenner and Tyga, boyfriend at the time, received an extravagant gift. It was an English Bulldog puppy named Rolly. The puppy came from Shrinkabulls.com which is known for providing celebrities with unique and very expensive breeds. Dogs from Shrinkabulls.com fall in the price range between $3,000 to $30,000 each. But, when Jenner posted Rolly on Snapchat she included dollar signs and the caption “50K.” Is that how much the dog was? He’s adorably scrunchy-faced and has tri-color spots, as a Merle English Bulldog.

Later that year, Kylie reported that the dog did not belong to her. It looks like the initial posts may have been from a short term visit. Or, perhaps the price tag was a little too pricey and they returned the dog.

Norman (Dog)

Breed: Italian miniature greyhound | Adopted Dec 2014

Kylie had said publicly several times that she always wanted an Italian greyhound. During Christmas 2014 she got her wish with Norman, a grey Italian Greyhound. Norman and Bambi are Kylie’s most popular dogs, and have their own Instagram accounts. Norman is affectionately known as “Normi” by Kylie and had one of the biggest dog birthday bashes ever for his first birthday. In 2016, Kylie had to miss the American Music Awards because Norman and Bambi had two surprise puppies!

Sophia (Dog)

Breed: Italian miniature greyhound | Adopted Sep 2015

Sofia is the third Italian greyhound added to Kylie’s collection, which she revealed in September 2015 on her Spanchat. She’s often dressed up alongside her greyhound siblings and allegedly lives a very boujiee life. One question we have though, why are Norman and Bambi more popular than Sophia? What did Sophia do?!

Ernie (Dog)

Breed: Long Haired Dachshund

Ernie, a little Dachshund, was first seen with the 18-year-old reality star, Kylie Jenner, posing outside of her $6 million mansion in Hidden Hills, California. Ernie is allegedly in a relationship with Kevin, the newest addition to the family. After Kylie introduced Kevin on her Snapchat, she can be heard asking Ernie if he is Kevin’s boyfriend. Ernie is Kylie’s fourth dog, adopting him on July 2016, right before she received Penny as a gift from ex-best-friend Jordyn Woods.

Adam (Dog)

Breed: Mix Breed | Adopted Sep 2012

Not much is known about puppy Adam, but he made his Instagram debut at the New York Fashion Week in September 2012.

Bambi (Dog)

Breed: Italian miniature greyhound | Adopted Mar 2015

Bambi joined Kylie’s greyhounds in March 2015. In December 2016, Bambi had puppies with Norman, Kylie had to keep the family together and so Harlie and Rosie joined the Jenner clan. We don’t know whether or not Kylie has opted to get Bambi spayed after the puppies, so there may be more adorable little Italian Greyhound puppies running around the celeb’s home in the future! 

More Bambi

Bruce (Bunny)

Breed: Bunny | Adopted Aug 2015

In August 2015, Kylie adopted a bunny and named it Bruce. It’s been assumed that the bunny was named after Bruce Jenner, who publicly announced his transition to Caitlyn Jenner two months earlier.

Wilbur (Pig)

Adopted Jul 2016

Kris Jenner gave Kylie a little brown piglet in July 2016, which was revealed in a deleted scene from Keeping Up With The Kardashians. The pig was named Wilbur by Kris and it’s unknown if Kylie kept or changed his name. When Kris first gave her the piglet, you can hear Kylie ask, “Is that a chicken?” in the video. Either she was so excited about the baby pig that she forgot what pigs were, or she has a difficult time telling farm animals apart. Since then, Kylie has followed up with this explanation, “Everyone keeps tweeting me this one Vine of my mom getting me a pig, and I mistake it for a chicken,” Kylie said on her Snapchat story in 2016. “But here’s the real story behind me thinking that it’s a chicken. So I love chickens. My mom asked me what I wanted for my housewarming gift, and I told her I want chickens. I want a chicken coop. I’m, like, dying to have a chicken. We had, like, over 10 conversations about it, so when I saw her downstairs with something all wrapped up in her arms, and I was upstairs, of course, I assumed that it was a f*cking chicken.”

Wilbur was re-homed, as he didn’t actually like the Jenner-Kardashians.

Louis & Vuitton (Dog)

Breed: Chocolate Labs | Adopted 2011

Louis and Vuitton were two chocolate labradors that Kendall and Kylie got as Christmas gifts in 2011 from Kris Jenner and Caitlyn Jenner. Kendall’s pup, a boy, is called Louis and Kylie’s, a girl, Vuitton. The two labradors have not been seen since 2012, and no one seems to know where they’ve ended up.

Bella (Dog)

Breed: Maltese

Bella is a Maltese that had been with the Kardashians for years, but now living with family friend Pastor Brad and his wife. No one is sure why Bella went to go live with Pastor Brad, and the mystery surrounding the Jenner-Kardashian pets seems to grow. We hope you’re happy with your new family, Bella!

Jackson (Dog)

Breed: Australian Shepherd Mix | Adopted 2012

In 2012, Kendall and Kylie rescued an Australian Shepherd mix as a gift for Khloe. They ended up keeping the dog themselves and naming it Jackson. It hasn’t been seen since 2012.

Kevin (Dog)

Breed: Italian Greyhound | Adopted Feb 2021

Kylie Jenner adopted her ninth dog named Kevin in February 2021. He’s a grey and white Italian Greyhound puppy and finally has a name worthy of the Keeping up with the Kardashian‘s cast. Kylie debuted Kevin to the world by posting two photos of him to her Instagram, introducing her 217 million followers to the dog with the simple caption, “kevin.”

Wesley (Dog)

Breed: Mixed Breed | Adopted Feb 2019

Jordyn Woods and Kylie Jenner adopted a puppy together named Wesley, in February 2019. Wesley’s breed is thought to be a Chihuahua-Dachshund mix. It is unsure who got custody as the best friends split up over Jordyn Woods’ and Tristan Thompson hooking up while he was dating Khloe Kardashian. Apparently, Wesley was seen poking around Jenner’s house in a few Instagram stories and cuddling with Jenner, indicating that she got custody of the pup in the friend-divorce, which makes sense, considering Jordyn Woods was living in her house when they adopted the dog together. Maybe Woods didn’t want to look for a pet-friendly apartment in LA.

Frosty (Mercy #2) (Cat)

Breed: British Shorthair | Adopted Dec 2021

Kylie Jenner got a British Shorthair kitten in December 2021 that she shared with her followers through her Instagram Stories. Kylie first said the cat’s name was Mercy #2, a tribute to Kim’s kitty Mercy who passed away. Then North West corrected her and said the cat’s name was Frosty.

Rosy and Harley (Dog)

Breed: Italian Greyhound | Adopted Nov 2016

Rosey and Harley are the puppies of Bambi and Norman, bringing Kylie’s total of Italian Greyhounds to seven! Bambi and Norman had the pups back in November 2016, to the surprise of their fur mom Kylie. Rosey and Harley were also the two Greyhounds Kylie’s baby daddy and rapper Travis Scott couldn’t name when Kylie interviewed him for GQ.

Odie (Dog)

Breed: Dachshund

Kylie Jenner has a long-haired dachshund named Odie. Odie went viral in 2016 when Kylie posted a video of him chewing on a $400 Fendi keychain, which caused the keychain to sell out internationally.

Jill (Jack and Jill) (Dog)

Breed: Italian Greyhound | Adopted Mar 2022

Kylie Jenner’s most recent and 7th Mini Italian Greyhound is named Jill, which she got in March 2023. Kylie and Kendall created some confusion when Kylie got Jill because both of them have referred to Jill as “Jack and Jill,” after the classic nursery rhyme. They led to many people thinking that she has a Greyhound named Jack, and with the number of Greyhounds she had, we can’t blame anyone for mixing them up (unless they also did an hour or so of research just to figure this out like me…)

Eddie (Chicken)

Breed: Silkie | Adopted Apr 2017

Kylie Jenner has a Silkie Bantam Chicken named Eddie that she got in April 2017. It followed her getting gifted a pig the previous year, when she really wanted a chicken. A Silkie is a fancy breed of chicken from China that has soft, fluffy feathers, instead of the usual hard feathers of your average chicken.

Breed: Dachshund | Adopted Nov 2023

Dolce (Dog)

Breed: Chihuahua | Adopted 2005

Dolce the Chihuahua was a family pet of the Kardashians and was seen on the early seasons of Keeping Up with the Kardashians. Dolce joined the Kardashian family around 2005, along with their Labrador Gabbana. Dolce went missing in 2012 and it was assumed a coyote killed him. Dolce is seen here with Kylie.

Gabbana (Dog)

Breed: Labrador | Adopted 2005

The Kardashian family got Gabbana in 2005, along with their late chihuahua Dolce. Gabbana was one of the longest-running Kardashian family pets. After her parent’s divorce, Khloe took Gabbana in. Gabbana unfortunately passed away in 2018. After her passing, Kris Jenner attempted to give Khloe three identical new puppies, but Khloe found the pups alternative homes. Apparently, no new puppies can fill Gabbana’s place in the Kardashian home. RIP Gabbana!

Frozen (Horse)

Breed: Expensive | Adopted Jul 2020

In July 2020, it was revealed that Kylie Jenner got a light grey pony named Frozen for her daughter Stormi, which cost around $200,000. Jenner was revealed as the buyer via the breeder, @stalwilten, posted the horse to Instagram and mentioned Kylie and Stormi. It was estimated to cost an additional $7,000 to $10,000 to fly the horse from the Netherlands to Los Angeles. The huge price tag coupled with the fact that Storm, only two years old at the time, wouldn’t be able to ride it for years, caused some controversy across social media. After the controversy, @stalwilten removed the reference to the Jenners.

ThrowbackThursday goes to the most famous pony of them all – Frozen – We got news he has landed in L.A. and living the life with a sweet little girl named Stormi. @kyliejenner in true fashion, made sure her daughter had the most precious pony out there. We can’t wait to see pictures of Stormi and Frozen.

Kylie Jenner

Birthday

August 10, 1997 (26)

Kylie Jenner is an American Reality TV star, entrepreneur, and social media influencer, widely known for her pivotal role on the reality TV show Keeping Up with the Kardashians, alongside her family members Kendall, Kris, Khloe, Kourtney, and Kim Kardashian.

Jenner’s journey to global recognition began with her family’s reality television series, Keeping Up with the Kardashians, which chronicled the personal and professional lives of the Kardashian-Jenner clan. Kylie grew up on the screen, and her candid depiction of adolescence struck a chord with viewers around the world. At the same time, Jenner made astute use of social media platforms, cultivating an enormous online following. This influence would later serve as a launchpad for her entrepreneurial endeavors. In 2015, she founded Kylie Cosmetics, introducing the now-iconic Kylie Lip Kit. Her makeup company rapidly grew into a cosmetics empire, with Forbes reporting it valued at nearly $900 million in 2019. In 2020, she delved into skincare, launching Kylie Skin, which further expanded her footprint in the beauty industry.

Jenner’s success is partially attributed to her knack for collaborations with other celebrities and influencers. She’s worked closely with her sisters, Kim Kardashian West and Kendall Jenner, to launch products under Kylie Cosmetics. In the realm of fashion, Kylie partnered with fashion house Balmain for a makeup collection in 2019. She also leveraged her massive online influence to promote brands like Puma and Adidas.

Over the course of her career, Jenner’s business acumen and influential status have brought her recognition in various forms. Forbes named her the youngest self-made billionaire in 2019, a testament to her success with Kylie Cosmetics. Her social media influence is also undeniable, with TIME including her in their list of “The 30 Most Influential Teens” for consecutive years 2014, 2015, and 2016.

Kendall is also known for her relationship with rapper Travis Scott, who she has two children with named Stormi and Aire Webster. In November 2023, Kylie admitted her “best hire” was a dog nanny for her 7 greyhounds.
