Mila Kunis, Ashton Kutcher Marry in Hush-Hush Wedding: Details!

 Posted on February 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  231 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Surprise! Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher are officially married, Us Weekly can confirm. The couple exchanged vows in a hush-hush ceremony at The Secret Garden at Parrish Ranch in Oak Glen, Calif. over the weekend.

Kunis and Kutcher first met on set of That '70s Show (he was her first kiss!) and began dating in spring 2011. The couple got engaged in February 2014 and welcomed daughter Wyatt that October.

The Ukrainian native, 31, and Kutcher, 37, have been brushing off marriage rumors for several months. Last New Year's Eve, Kutcher sparked speculation when he signed a Facebook post with "The Kutchers." The Jupiter Ascending actress also played coy about possibly saying "I do" during The Late Late Show in March.

"I never wanted to get married. From the age of 12, I prepared my parents for no marriage," Kunis told W magazine's August 2014 issue. "Then things changed — I found the love of my life. Now my theory on weddings is: Don't invite anyone. Do it privately and secretly. My parents are okay with that."
