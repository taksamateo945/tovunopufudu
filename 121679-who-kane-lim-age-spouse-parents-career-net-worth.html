Kane Lim’s biography

Lim is a Singaporean living in Los Angeles, and it has been his home for about nine years. He is a vegetarian and turned to Buddhism three years ago. Unlike rich kids, he used public transport to school and preferred Singaporean hawker food to fancy meals.

Lim admitted that his maternal grandmother makes the best yusheng and enjoys spending time with family because they get him through everything.

His interest in fashion began at a young age because it helped him overcome his weight insecurities. Kids made fun of him in school for being fat. Kane has a positive attitude towards his childhood insecurity because it makes him compassionate.

What does Kane Lim's family do?

Kane Lim’s family is very wealthy, having ventured into the shipping, oil, and real estate industries. It is believed that his dad is Kenny Lim Oon Cheng, who controls a billion-dollar company and is a board member of Sian Chay Medical Institution.

The organization promotes Traditional Chinese Medicine in Singapore. In addition, Kenny's brother, Lim Oon Kuin, is the founder of Hin Leong Trading (HLT). The company filed for bankruptcy in 2020.

Kane Lim's family drama in Singapore

In 2020, it was reported that one of Lim’s extended family members was engaging in fraudulent activities. The family's $3.5 billion (in assets) was frozen when investigations kicked off, and some of the businesses have been liquidated.

How old is Kane Lim?

Kane Lim's age is 32 years as of 2023. He was born on December 5, 1990, in Singapore. His zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

Educational background

Lim studied Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) Merchandise Marketing at the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM) from 2010 to 2013.

Career

Talking about his career, his father gave him a loan when he moved to the US to further his studies and expand his business. The then-teenager quickly repaid the money and made his first $1 million before his 20th birthday.

The Bling Empire series took Lim to international stardom. His father was the first to support his onscreen career, while the other family members were warming up to the idea.

He uses the Bling Empire to highlight important things in society and wants to grow his TV career and make a more fun reality show. He and co-cast Kim Lee (Vietnamese-American model and DJ) are good friends. They first made contact with each other over Instagram in 2019 before featuring together in Bling Empire.

He has also been friends with Rihanna for over eight years. She follows him on Instagram and was the first Asian to be the brand ambassador of Rihanna's Fentyskin and beauty brand.

Kane has also appeared on the Ellen DeGeneres talk show and A California Christmas: City Lights and attended the opening of L.A. Fashion Week.

How is Kane Lim rich?

Kane co-founded Leighton&Kane in December 2012 and established an online fashion store called Thefashionfund. He used the fashion business' earnings to start three charities (St Jude's, Red Cross Singapore, and Holly Grove) in January 2014.

Lim currently operates a Los Angeles investment firm. The company deals in renewable energy, hospitality, real estate, and health and wellness. He is also the director of an investment business known as K-ix Capital.

Kane is interested in real estate because he loves tangible assets. He works for Selling Sunset’s Oppenheim Group brokerage and has a license. He saves the 2.5% real estate agent commission by representing himself in personal deals.

One of his greatest real estate achievements was developing luxurious properties in Venice Beach, Los Angeles. Additionally, the business magnate sells exclusive and expensive properties. For example, he closed an $8.5 million house in Malibu with a friend.

The entrepreneur has many investments in America and Singapore, including commercial and residential properties and fancy shopping malls in Southeast Asia (i.e., Singapore and Thailand).

He has invested in Happy Fish (a swim school for kids, adolescents, and senior citizens), Burgreens (an Indonesian plant-based company), and its subsidiary Green Rebel.
