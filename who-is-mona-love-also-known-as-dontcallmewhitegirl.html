Who Is Mona Love and Where is She From?

Mona Love is between 25 to 35 years old. Her nationality is American while her ethnicity is mixed, with major elements of African-American and possibly Caucasian thrown in. Mona Love’s real name is Damona. She is however popularly known as Dontcallmewhitegirl i.e. don’t call me white girl, and the inspiration behind the name is obvious.

Mona Love is quite fair in complexion and this must have led to comments about her heritage. This would have also led many of her peers to dub her white girl. Such comments would in turn have led her to push back and ask people to not call her a white girl. Therefore, Mona Love is called Dontcallmewhitegirl because she doesn’t want to be referred to as a white girl as a result of her complexion.

She Spent Time in Jail for Fraud in her Late Teens

There is a paucity of information about Mona Love’s family. It is however known that she comes from poor family background and grew up in a poor neighborhood in Philadelphia. This was quite depressing. Even though Love was naturally talented and bright from a young age, her environment made her doubt her ability to achieve something tangible. Child prostitutes and drug users were a common sight in her neighborhood and this demoralized her.

She, therefore, became misguided as a youth and didn’t take school seriously. She eventually found herself in jail in her late teens for fraud. Mona Love was released from jail at the age of 21. She tried to pursue a legitimate way of life but this was made difficult by the fact that the system was unfavorable to ex-convicts. This made for some bleak years in her life.

She Started her Dontcallmewhitegirl Podcast in 2018

Throughout the bleak years of being in jail and dealing with life as an ex-convict, one thing that Mona Love never lost was her capacity to make people laugh. She hesitated at first but she later commenced her Dontcallmewhitegirl podcast in 2018. Mona Love’s podcast continues to thrive to this day. She usually offers her opinions on a wide range of topics including politics, pop culture, relationships, and dating. However, after the whole thing was up, Mona Love visited her love interest and tried to rekindle the flame. He however shot her down and questioned her as to why she was being weird. Love’s portrayal of the whole thing was so funny but the icing on the cake was when she mimicked her beau uttering the words – why you being weird to me?

Her Twitter account has 65.9k followers while her Tik Tok account has at least 65k followers. Mona Love also had a large engagement on Instagram but she lost it after her account was banned.
