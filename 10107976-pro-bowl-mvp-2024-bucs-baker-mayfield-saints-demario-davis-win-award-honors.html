Baker Mayfield and Demario Davis will be taking home some hardware, as the two were named the offensive and defensive MVP of the 2024 NFL Pro Bowl.

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers quarterback and the New Orleans Saints linebacker both delivered strong performances during a competitive Sunday afternoon, with the NFC defeating the AFC 64-59 in the Pro Bowl Games.

Although head coach Peyton Manning's team dominated the flag football portion of the afternoon by winning 50-34, the NFC's supremacy in the team skills competitions gave the conference an edge in the overall score.

Mayfield was responsible for the conference's victory in the Precision Passing challenge on Thursday, as he out-dueled Houston Texans signal-caller C.J. Stroud in the final round to give the NFC three points.
