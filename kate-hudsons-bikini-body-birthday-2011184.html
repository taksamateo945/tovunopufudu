Kate Hudson's Bikini Body Through the Years: Photos

 Posted on May 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  402 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Bikini babe! Kate Hudson isn’t afraid to flaunt her swimsuit ready body by the pool, on the beach or on camera.

The Almost Famous star frequently shows off her figure in two-pieces while soaking up the sun year-round at her home in Los Angeles. Plus, she rocks a variety of bikinis whenever she travels abroad — and Us Weekly rounded up her best and chicest suit choices over the years.

“I do love a good bikini,” Hudson told InStyle during her July 2014 cover story interview. “And I think it’s because I just love the sun so much.”

The How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days actress added: “I love summer, it’s my favorite season. I love being by the water!”

Hudson explained that she often tops off her bikini look with a hat for sun protection. “Accessorizing is always fun to me,” she told the outlet. “And during summer when I’m outside a lot or at the beach, my face tends to get easily pigmented even if I’m wearing a ton of sunblock — so I throw on a cute hat!”

The Oscar nominee is also a fan of highlighting her assets in support of her numerous business ventures, including her liquor brand, King St. Vodka.

“It’s #NationalBikiniDay! Or, as we like to call it, National Drink in Your Bikini Day!” the businesswoman wrote via Instagram in June 2022 while posing in a pink two-piece poolside with a cocktail close by. “I’ll cheers to that 🙌.”

Later that year, the “Sibling Revelry” podcast cohost again showed off her toned physique while wearing a bright orange bikini in Netflix’s Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery.

In the movie, the actress, who played socialite Birdie Jay, strutted poolside in the skimpy outfit, which she accessorized with orange high heels, jewelry and a floppy hat.
