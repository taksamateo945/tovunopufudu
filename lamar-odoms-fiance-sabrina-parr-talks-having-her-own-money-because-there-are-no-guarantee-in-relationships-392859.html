Lamar Odom’s net worth is estimated to be around $30 million. However, Sabrina Parr will keep living the same lifestyle that she has been until they tie the knot.

The personal trainer has been engaged to the athlete since last year — not without drama. Between being accused of domestic abuse by Lamar’s daughter and looked at as a gold digger by the public, Sabrina has already been through the wringer in her relationship.

Parr had her own business and money before meeting the former Khloe and Lamar star. She took to social media o make it clear that she will be making no major lifestyle changes until they wed.

Her Instagram Story message read: ‘Here’s a little fact about me. I will continue to live within MY means until I am married! Wherever I live or whatever I drive, I spend my own money on it and I make sure it’s something I can afford comfortably on my own and my name is included! Lamar and I are not married yet nor do we have children together. There are no guarantees in relationships and I never want to be in a position again where something happens and Im stuck financially. He could leave me today and I’d be good on my own. Once we get married, he can spend away and imma put my coins up lol!’
