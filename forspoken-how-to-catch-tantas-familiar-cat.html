Tanta’s familiars are unique cat-like creatures that can be found all around the open world of Forspoken.

To catch Tanta’s familiar cat, players will need to approach them slowly and make sure not to scare them off, as indicated by the yellow exclamation mark symbol.

How to Find Familiar Monuments in Forspoken

These monuments can be found all over the open world and are symbolized by a Paw symbol. Simply open your map and if you’ve passed by one, it will be marked with a paw symbol.

If you can’t see any, keep reading as I’ll mention all familiar monument locations in the game.

How to Catch Tanta’s Familiar Cat in Forspoken

If you’ve found one of Tanta’s familiar cats in the game, the process of catching them is fairly straightforward. Here is everything you need to do:

  • Crouch by pressing L3 on PlayStation or right-clicking on PC.
  • Start approaching the familiar as slowly as possible, only taking one step at a time.
  • When a yellow exclamation mark symbol appears on top of the familiar, stop moving completely.
  • When the symbol disappears, continue moving toward it, again as slowly as possible.
  • Repeat until you get next to it and are given the option to pet it.

    • If you fail to approach the familiar and scare it off by moving when the exclamation mark symbol appears, don’t worry. Simply leave the location and travel back, and the familiar will be back on its spot.
