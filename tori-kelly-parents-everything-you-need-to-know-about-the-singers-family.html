Tori Kelly is a Grammy-winning singer-songwriter who rose to fame after posting videos on YouTube as a teenager.

She has released four studio albums, voiced Meena in the animated film Sing and its sequel, and collaborated with artists like Ed Sheeran, Pharrell Williams, and Amy Grant.

But who are Tori Kelly’s parents, and how did they influence her musical journey?

Here’s everything you need to know about the singer’s family.

Who are Tori Kelly’s parents?

Tori Kelly  parents are Allwyn and Laura Kelly.

They met in the 1980s when they were both musicians in a band called Exodus.

Allwyn is of Afro-Jamaican and Puerto Rican ancestry, while Laura is of Irish and German descent.

They have a younger son named Noah, who is also a musician.

Kelly also revealed that her parents’ divorce after 24 years of marriage served as inspiration for one of her albums, Inspired by True Events .

Tori Kelly songs

Tori Kelly is a singer and songwriter who has many popular songs in different genres and styles. Some of them are:
