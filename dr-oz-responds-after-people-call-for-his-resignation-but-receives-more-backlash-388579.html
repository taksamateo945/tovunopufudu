Dr. Oz Responds After People Call For His Resignation, But Receives More Backlash

 Posted on May 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  314 words  |  Martina Birk

Dr. Oz found himself in hot water not too long ago, after he made some comments, saying that schools should reopen. He said all this and more on Fox News, and now, he’s back with an explanation after people called for his resignation.

Check out the videos that The Shade Room re-posted.

People are not showing him any support as you will see in the comments below.

A fan defended his and said: ‘Y’all leave OZ alone y’all know that man doesn’t bother anyone,’ but someone else said: ‘Was over him after he said don’t wash your chicken.’
